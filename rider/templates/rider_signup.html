Thank you for submitting your application to become a Fele Express partner driver. Your application has been received and here is the next step in your journey to start earning with us.

Upload Your Documentary Requirements

Now that you have completed your initial app registration, upload the required documents based on the vehicle type you’ll be using. These include:

  • Professional Driver’s License - This is required for four-wheel vehicle type only
  • NIN or National ID or any other government issued ID
  • Official Receipt (OR), Certificate of Registration (CR), and Deed of Sale (or letter of authorization if not owned) of the Vehicle
  • Photos of the Vehicle (front, side, and back with clear plate number)
  • Profile Photo (2 X 2 )

If you do not have soft copies of these documents to upload in the app, you may bring hard copies to the office during your training.




Attend Your Training and Orientation

This is compulsory after registration.

Your Fele Express team will get you informed about the company philosophy and policies, how to use the mobile app, how you should deal with your clients, and many other things that you should know and learn being a Fele partner driver.

Settle Refundable Cash Bonds

Before you become a verified and official Fele partner driver, there are cash bonds and small fees for startup kits that you must settle. These include:

  • NGN2,000 (plus NGN500 worth of load wallet and NGN250 for kit) cash bond for Motorcycle and Keke Partner Drivers.
  • NGN5,000 (plus NGN1000 worth of load wallet and NGNP350 for kit) cash bond for Cars, MPV and Light Truck Partner Drivers.

These cash bonds are refundable after 2 months with Fele Express provided you have not broken any rules or policies.
Every qualified driver or rider will be given 2 Fele Express shirts, one power bank, one motorcycle phone holder, dispatch box (For select riders), brochure, and driver handbook as starter kits.
